Privacy Policy

Spit · Last updated: April 2025

The short version

Spit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. We have no servers. Your voice and your text stay between your Mac and OpenAI.

What Spit does

Spit is a macOS dictation app. When you press the shortcut and speak, your audio is sent directly from your Mac to OpenAI's Whisper API using your own API key. The transcribed text is then typed into whatever app you are using.

Data we collect

None. Spit does not have analytics, crash reporting, or any network connection other than the OpenAI API call made with your own key.

Data OpenAI collects

When you use Spit, your audio is processed by OpenAI. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle your data. By default, OpenAI does not use API data to train models.

Your API key

Your OpenAI API key is stored exclusively in the macOS Keychain on your device. It is never transmitted to us or any third party other than OpenAI's API endpoint.

Permissions

  • Microphone — required to capture your voice for transcription. Audio is sent directly to OpenAI and is not stored locally beyond the duration of the transcription request.
  • Accessibility — required to type the transcribed text into other apps on your Mac. This permission is used locally only.

In-app purchase

Spit Pro is a one-time purchase processed by Apple. We do not receive your payment information. Apple's privacy practices apply to the purchase transaction.
